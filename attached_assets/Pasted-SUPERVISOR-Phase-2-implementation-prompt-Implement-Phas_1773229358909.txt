Each constraint sent to Tower must carry an evidence_requirement field declaring what kind of proof is needed. Use this mapping:

NAME_CONTAINS, NAME_STARTS_WITH → none (self-evident, verifiable from lead data)
LOCATION → directory_data (location should be confirmed from a directory or maps source)
COUNT_MIN → none (arithmetic, no evidence needed)
HAS_ATTRIBUTE → website_text (requires first-party website confirmation)

If you cannot determine the right tier, default to external_source.

first_party_website — evidence from the business's own domain
search_snippet — evidence from a Google/search result snippet
directory_field — evidence from a structured directory field (Google Places, Yelp etc.)
lead_field — evidence from a field on the lead record itself (name, address, phone)
external_source — evidence from a third-party page (review site, aggregator)

Map your existing source_type values to these tiers. Do not discard the original source_type — keep both.
