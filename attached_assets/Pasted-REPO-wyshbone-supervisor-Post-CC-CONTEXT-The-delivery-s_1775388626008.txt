REPO: wyshbone-supervisor-Post-CC

CONTEXT: The delivery_summary artefact correctly produces PASS/PARTIAL/STOP/ERROR statuses. But the agent_run record always gets `terminalState: 'completed'` regardless of what the Tower verdict was. This means anything checking the run status (UI, AFR, analytics) sees "completed" even when the run was stopped by Tower or failed verification.

FIX: Derive the agent_run terminalState from the delivery summary status instead of always using 'completed'.

Mapping:
- Delivery PASS or PARTIAL → terminalState: 'completed' (results were delivered)
- Delivery STOP → terminalState: 'stopped' (Tower or system stopped the run)
- Delivery ERROR → terminalState: 'failed'
- Delivery CHANGE_PLAN → terminalState: 'completed' (still in progress, plan was changed)
