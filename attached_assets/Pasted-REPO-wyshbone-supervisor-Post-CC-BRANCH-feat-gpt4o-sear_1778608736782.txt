CONTEXT
The user-visible 4-bullet response says "All N verified with on-page evidence"
even when Tower's verdict is fail/stop/error. The verifiedCount value passed in
is the supervisor's pre-Tower optimistic count (cvl_verified_exact_count) and
never gets reset when Tower judges the run as a failure. The response builder
treats verifiedCount and towerVerdict as independent inputs.

GOAL
Make verifiedCount honest at the top of response-builder by reconciling it
against towerVerdict. If Tower failed/stopped/errored, no leads were truly
verified — regardless of what the supervisor's optimistic count said.
